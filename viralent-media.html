FAQ

Questions We Get
All the Time

How long does a campaign take to go live?
Meme campaigns can go live in 48–72 hours. Influencer campaigns typically take 5–10 days from briefing to the first post going live, depending on creator availability and content review time.
Do I need to pay upfront?
Yes, we require 50–100% advance depending on the package. This covers creator fees and platform costs. For larger campaigns, we can discuss a 50% upfront / 50% on-delivery structure.
Can I choose which creators work on my campaign?
Absolutely. We share a shortlist of 2–3x the required creators and let you approve who's selected. We guide you on who we think is the best fit, but the final call is always yours.
What if I'm not happy with the content?
All content is reviewed by us before publishing. If it doesn't match the brief, we send it back to the creator for revision. Every package includes at least 1–2 rounds of revisions.
How do you report results?
You receive a PDF campaign report with screenshots and metrics including total reach, impressions, engagement rate, story views, link clicks, and a per-creator breakdown.
Do you work with very small brands or new startups?
Yes — in fact, early-stage brands are some of our favourite clients. We can build campaigns starting from ₹5,000–8,000 and scale from there as you grow and see results.
What niches do you specialise in?
Fashion, D2C, fintech apps, gaming, health & wellness, food, personal brands, and local businesses. If your category isn't listed, just ask — our creator network is broad.
Can you manage creator sponsorships if I'm an influencer?
Yes. We work with creators across 10K–500K+ followers to source, pitch, negotiate, and close brand deals — and handle follow-ups and payments. Reach out via WhatsApp to discuss.
